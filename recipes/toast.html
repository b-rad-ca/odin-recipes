Toast

Description

Toast is a dish best served with breakfast, as it's sad when eaten in the evening

Ingredients

  • Un-toasted bread
  • Spread or dips of choice

Steps

  1. Add un-toasted bread to a toaster, select brownness setting and turn on
  2. Once complete, check for desiered browning
  3. Add desiered spread, or serve with your dip of choice
