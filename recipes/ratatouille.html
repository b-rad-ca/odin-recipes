Ratatouille

Description

Ratatouille is a French dish originating in Nice, consisting of stewed vegetables, aromatics including garlic and onion, along with herbs native to the region.

Ingredients

  • 1 small eggplant, trimmed and thinly sliced
  • 1 zuchini, trimmed and thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow squash, trimmed and thinly sliced
  • 1 yeallow and 1 red pepper, cored and thinly sliced
  • 1 can (6 oz) tomato past
  • half onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp fresh thyme leaes
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3/4 cup water
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat a large pan on medium heat. You'll know it's warm enough when a drop of water immedialty evaporates from the surface
  2. Add olive oil to coat the pan, and add onions and garlic. Cook until onions are translucent, but not browned
  3. Reduce heat to medium low. Add all vegetabes, half of the fresh thyme and cook for 2 minutes
  4. Add tomato paste and water, simmer 5 minutes
  5. Move ingredients to an oven safe container and cook on 350F for 15 minutes
  6. Add salt and pepper to tast, garnish with remaining thyme and serve
