Eggs

Description

Breakfast? Lunch? Dinner?

Eggs are egg-cellent any time!

Ingredients

  • 1-3 eggs
  • Butter
  • 1oz water
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Add butter to a pan over medum heat, until butter begins to gently bubble
  2. Crack in eggs
  3. Once the whites are cooked on the bottom but still translucent on the top, add 1oz of water and cover your pan
  4. Turn off stove, allowing risidual heat and steam to finish cooking eggs
  5. Season with salt and pepper to tast
